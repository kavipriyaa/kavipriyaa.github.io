Kurukshetra, an International Techno - Management Festival initiated by CEG Tech Forum in the year 2007, is the first collegiate festival in India to be honored with the UNESCO patronage. True to its tagline, The Battle of Brains, Kurukshetra in its seventh edition, slated to be held from January 30th - February 2nd 2013.Armed with challenging events, workshops that redefine learning, cutting-edge research and technology from across the globe and lectures from world- renowned personalities, from the academia and the industry, Kurukshetra perpetually strives to find solutions to the various challenges facing the world.
